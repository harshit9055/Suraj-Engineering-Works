About Us

With over a decade of experience, Suraj Engineering Works has built a solid reputation for expertise in civil engineering projects focused on the energy and utilities sectors. From designing and constructing petrol pumps to the establishment and maintenance of electric power stations, we are committed to delivering exceptional quality and dependable service. Our team of skilled professionals works closely with partners and clients, ensuring each project meets the highest industry standards for safety, efficiency, and sustainability. Our partnership with HPCL and other industry leaders has enabled us to stay at the forefront of engineering innovation, using advanced techniques and materials to enhance durability and efficiency. We pride ourselves on meticulous planning, precise execution, and an unwavering dedication to client satisfaction. At Suraj Engineering Works, reliability isn’t just a goal—it’s the foundation of our legacy and the key to our ongoing success in delivering sustainable, high-performance engineering solutions..
